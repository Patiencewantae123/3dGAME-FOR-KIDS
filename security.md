## ğŸ” Security Measures

To ensure the safety and security of our users, we implement the following practices:

- **Data Privacy**:
  - No personal data collection.
  - No online interactions or chat functionalities.
  - No third-party advertisements or tracking tools.

- **Code Audits**:
  - Regularly review the codebase for potential vulnerabilities.
  - Automated and manual testing to identify weaknesses.

- **Dependency Management**:
  - Use only trusted libraries and frameworks.
  - Keep dependencies updated to patch known vulnerabilities.

- **Platform Security**:
  - Ensure builds comply with platform security guidelines (e.g., sandboxing for mobile apps).
